# Privacy

The speed-test endpoint moves temporary bytes for measurement. It does not persist payloads.

## What stays local

Recent result history is stored in the browser's local storage so you can compare runs from the same device.

## What the endpoint receives

The endpoint receives normal request metadata and temporary upload payloads. Uploads are capped and discarded after the request.

## What not to infer

buffer.lol measures a browser-to-server path, not your full ISP contract. Your result may differ from provider tests, router tests, or tests to a closer server.
