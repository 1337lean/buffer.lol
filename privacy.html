Privacy notice

Alpha waitlist privacy.

buffer.lol collects the email address you submit so we can manage alpha access and send waitlist updates.

What We Collect

  • Your email address from the waitlist form.
  • Basic technical metadata collected by the form host, such as submission time, IP address, user agent, and spam-detection signals.
  • Browser-local demo data for the simulated probe and local waitlist preview when you run the site locally.

How We Use It

We use waitlist submissions to contact people about alpha access, product updates, and related buffer.lol preview communication. We do not sell waitlist emails.

Local Demo Data

The simulated diagnostics probe stores preview runs in your browser's local storage. That data stays on your device unless you export or share it.

Removal Requests

To ask for removal from the waitlist, email hello@buffer.lol from the address you submitted.
